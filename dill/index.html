PLACES TO VISIT

The places you can visit

Nachchimale Temple

Madakada Aranya Senasanaya is located in Nachchimale. On Poya Days and Weekends big crowds are visiting the Senasanaya for worship and a water stream passing by the side of the Aranya, makes a peaceful environment for meditation.

Seethawaka Wet Zone Botanical Garden

The latest addition to the 5 botanical gardens in Sri Lanka, the Seethawaka Wet Zone Botanical Garden in Avissawella was opened to the public towards the latter part of the year 2014. The drive from Colombo takes approximately an hour or slightly more. This wet zone botanical garden covers a land area of 79 acres while the climate is quite cool and temperate.

Leisure World Water Park

Leisure World Water Park - Seethawaka is a wonderful place to enjoy your free time. It is a standard water park in Sri Lanka and it allows you to do so many water related activties. This water park is situated in 26km away from our hotel.
