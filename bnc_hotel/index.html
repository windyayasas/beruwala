ACTIVITIES

Activities to do and places to visit

Beruwala Harbour

Beruwala Harbour is one of the main fishery harbours on the western coast of Sri Lanka. It is situated in the southern edge of the Kalutara district, which is about 60 km south of Colombo. It has the capability of supplying berthing, refrigeration and fuel facilities which are often needed by fishing boats.

Beruwala Beach

Beruwala is located in the south west cost of Sri Lanka about 60 km from Colombo. Beach is a 130 km stretch of beach in a small tranquil fishing village and it is treated as the starting point of the southern cost beach resorts. The sea is excellent for swimming all year around.

Beruwala Light House

Beruwela lighthouse is located on Barberyn Island on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka. The light goes on from 6.30 pm until 6.30 am. The light rotates for one minute each time and directs sailors.

Aluthgma Kande Viharaya

Kande Viharaya in Aluthgama has the tallest sitting Buddha statue in the world at about 48m / 160ft in height. The building of the Kande Viharaya was initiated way back in 1734 by the Ven. Dedduwa Dhammananda Thero, an understudy of the most Ven. Weliwita Sri Saranankara Nayaka Thero. Thousands of devotees flock the holy grounds of the Kande Viharaya daily, seeking mental & spiritual relief from all the troubles & worries of day to day life.
